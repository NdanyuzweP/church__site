Reformed Baptist Church (RBC) , Masizi is a church for Kigali community, committed to the Reformed Faith! We would love to have you as our guest! We currently meet every Wednesday from 6.00 PM to 7.00 PM for Prayers and Sunday from 9.00 AM to 4.30 PM for a Common Worship physically. We also meet online every Thursday from 8.00 PM to 9.00 PM for a Sermon, and Friday from 7.30 PM to 9.00 PM for a Bible Study.

We are a church that takes the Bible seriously. We are committed to historic, Biblical worship in the Reformed tradition. We have expository (verse by verse, Biblical) preaching each week, hold to confessional Reformed theology (1689 2nd Landon Baptist Confession of faith), have a plurality of elders, and are passionate about evangelism, missions, Biblical counseling, and seeing our community changed with the Gospel. Kids are welcome to join us for worship!.
